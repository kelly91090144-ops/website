關於我們About Us

彰化走向世界From Changhua to the World

續發國際貿易有限公司（Constant Fortune International）成立於1992年，位於台灣彰化市，是台灣最具規模的材料製造與出口商之一。

Founded in 1992, Constant Fortune International Co., Ltd. is headquartered in Changhua City, Taiwan, and stands as one of the largest material manufacturers and exporters in the country.

我們不只是材料供應商——我們是全球時尚供應鏈的策略夥伴，供應給世界知名的鞋子品牌各式材料，除此之外也供應包包、衣服、家具製品，是一家穩健成長，具國際觀的知名企業，共同打造世界級的產品。

We are more than a materials supplier — we are a strategic partner in the global fashion supply chain, providing diverse materials to the world's leading footwear brands, as well as bags, apparel, and furniture products. A steadily growing, internationally-minded enterprise co-creating world-class products.

秉持「誠信第一、永續經營」的核心理念，我們在超過60個國家建立穩固的合作關係，以品質和信用贏得客戶超過三十年的信任。

Guided by the principles of integrity and sustainable growth, we have built enduring partnerships across more than 60 countries, earning client trust through consistent quality and reliability for over thirty years.

台版金皇宮Taiwan Golden Palace HQ
企業總部Our Headquarters

融合日本、德國、義大利建材精華，歷時五年精心打造的「台版金皇宮」，是我們招待全球合作夥伴的驕傲舞台。整棟建築具備頂級耐震、防火、防水等科技環保功能，流線型金色裝飾與水晶燈飾貫穿全樓，盡顯摩登奢華品味。

Our headquarters, the "Taiwan Golden Palace," was meticulously crafted over five years, integrating premium building materials from Japan, Germany, and Italy. State-of-the-art seismic, fire, and waterproofing technologies, adorned with fluid gold finishes and crystal lighting — a proud stage for our global partners.

董事長介紹About the President

從彰化出發，走向世界From Changhua to the Global Stage

「我只要講出來的一定做到，說到做到，這是我最重要的原則。」 "Whatever I say, I will do. My word is my commitment — that is my most important principle."

蔡慧惠，人稱「天后」，為人親切和善、重視信用。她以真誠與熱情對待每一位合作夥伴，讓許多客戶不只是生意夥伴，更成為最好的朋友。

她不只是成功的企業家，更熱心投入公益活動——關懷社區老人、為學校建設圖書館，用行動實踐「因為我，讓周邊的人更好」的人生哲學。

蔡慧惠相信，為人說到做到、誠信經營，才是企業經營的不二法門。她根留台灣，生意遍及全球60多個國家，持續提升鞋材等材料的國際影響力與重要性。

Jinny Tsai, affectionately known as "The Queen," is warm, approachable, and deeply values trustworthiness. Her genuine enthusiasm transforms every business partner into a lifelong friend.

Beyond business, she is deeply committed to social causes — caring for elderly community members and funding school libraries — living her philosophy: "Because of me, the people around me become better."

Jinny believes that keeping one's word and operating with integrity is the only way to run a business. Rooted in Taiwan, her business spans more than 60 countries worldwide, continuously elevating the international influence of shoe materials and beyond.
